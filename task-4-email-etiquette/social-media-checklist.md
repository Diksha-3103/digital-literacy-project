# Responsible Social Media Checklist
## Do’s
1. Audit your privacy settings regularly. Ensure personal posts are visible only to friends and family.
2. Use a professional profile picture. On platforms like LinkedIn or your portfolio, use a high-quality, clear headshot.
3. Fact-check before you share. In an era of AI-generated content, verify news from reliable sources before hitting "repost."

## Don’ts
1. Don’t post in the heat of the moment. Avoid venting about professors, bosses, or classmates while angry.
2. Don’t share sensitive personal info. Keep your student ID, home address, and current exact location private.
3. Don’t ignore your "tagged" photos. Untag yourself from unprofessional photos or set your profile to "Review Tags."
4. Don’t engage with "trolls" or cyberbullies. It rarely ends well and can make you look unprofessional to outsiders.
5. Don’t post illegal or policy-violating content. This includes underage drinking or anything that breaks your school's code of conduct.
